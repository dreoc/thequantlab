The QuantLab

Our research investigates human evolution do—across time, environments, and evolutionary contexts. We approach this question through quantitative modeling that bridges AI and other computational and data sciences with anthropology, evolutionary ecology, and human behavioral biology.

At its core, the QuantLab develops integrates analytical and computational methods to test causal hypotheses about human behavior, ecology, and morphology. We build models that formalize anthropological theory, simulate ecological and energetic processes, and statistically evaluate their predictions against archaeological and ethnographic data.
